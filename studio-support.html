Purpose

Practical audio help without making it complicated

Studio Support is an additional Leaning Studios service lane. It does not replace recording, mix/master, or full-service production. It gives local customers and professional partners a clear path for audio setup, routing, workflow, troubleshooting, and project-support problems.
